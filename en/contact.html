Privacy Policy

Saizuki Tsukushi (hereinafter referred to as "this website") handles personal information provided through inquiries in accordance with the following policy.

1. Personal Information Collected

Through the inquiry form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your inquiry.

2. Purpose of Use

Personal information collected will be used solely for the purpose of responding to and communicating regarding your inquiry. We will not provide your personal information to any third party without your consent.

3. Management of Personal Information

Personal information collected is managed within Google Workspace with appropriate security measures in place. After the inquiry has been addressed, the information will be promptly deleted or anonymized.

4. Inquiries

For questions regarding the handling of personal information, please contact us using the form above.
